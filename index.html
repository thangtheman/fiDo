Z Living’s new show Finding Fido is all about matchmaking—only instead of matching up people, show host Seth Casteel pairs people with pooches, working to find the perfect pet for his guests between three shelter animals in need of a home. Ultimately, one lucky dog finds his forever home, and one lucky person finds his new best friend.

Meet Seth Casteel,
He Really, Really Loves Dogs

So much that he’s willing to get wet with hundreds of ’em. Casteel, a photographer, became an overnight sensation when his rowdy, rollicking action shots of dogs underwater went viral. Those photos refocused the path of his life: He turned his initial photo collection into the New York Times bestselling book, Underwater Dogs, followed by the (even cuter) photo book, Underwater Puppies. Along the way, he worked with hundreds of shelter dogs, which taught him how the right image can lead directly to an adoption. ...Read More
